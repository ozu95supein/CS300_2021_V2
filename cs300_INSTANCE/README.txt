 Move the camera around in always looking at the object.
▪ W: Move up.
▪ S: Move down.
▪ A: Move left.
▪ D: Move right.
▪ E: Further from object.
▪ Q: Closer to object.
 +: Increase the number of grass objects in the area
 -: Decrease the number of grass objects in the area
